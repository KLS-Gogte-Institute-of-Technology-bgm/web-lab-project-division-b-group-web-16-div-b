 Torrentium Lodge

 Torrentium Lodge is a heritage property more than 100 years old and can be found in the heart of Shimla, Queen of the Hills. The  Governor's House is 800 metres from the lodge.Rooms boast original British-colonial interiors with beautiful wooden floors, ceilings,  windows and doors. Conveniences include cable TV, tea/coffee-maker and a private bathroom. Upscale rooms add a Jacuzzi and rain fall  showers. The property offers free on-site parking as well as useful amenities like a laundry service, car rental, travel information and a  Doctor on call. The hotel's multi-cuisine Moti Mahal Bar & Restaurant offers an a la carte menu and delicious Indian cuisine. Breakfast is  served in the rooms or out on the terrace. The famous Hindu Temple, Jakhu is four kilometres from Torrentium Lodge, with the prominent  Christ Church about five kilometres away. Located on Mall Road, the State Museum is just more than a five-kilometre drive from the hotel.
